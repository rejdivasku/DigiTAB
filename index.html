Features

Create digital ads through ready-made templates

Create stunning digital ads effortlessly: Say goodbye to the hassle and expense of hiring a designer. Our platform allows you to generate digital advertising quickly and easily using our ready-made templates and WYSIWYG editor. With the ability to fully customize your layouts and templates, you can promote your products and communicate with your customers like never before. Plus, our proven marketing materials provide your business with a professional image that will set you apart from the competition.

Target the audience based on location

Target your audience based on location: With our innovative technology, you can manage how your ads are displayed in each location to provide diverse material to your clients. Our platform allows you to control your communications with your customers based on their location, ensuring that your advertising is always relevant and targeted.

Schedule ads in advance

Schedule your ads in advance: Whether your digital sign is in the next room or across the country, our platform makes it easy to schedule your ads in advance. With full customization and control over display order and screen time for each of your ads, you can combine the content of ads showing videos and posters on split screens for maximum impact.

Combine Live TV with text/images

Combine Live TV with text/images: Keep your customers engaged and informed with our innovative split-screen feature. Show live TV in one part of the screen while displaying text or images in the other. This unique feature allows you to create a truly immersive experience for your customers, keeping them engaged and informed about your products and services.

What is Needed

DigiTAB PLATFORM

This web-based tool lets you schedule and fully control the order and screen time of your ads, giving you complete flexibility and control over your digital signage. DIGITAL SIGNAGE DEVICE

To bring your content to life, we also provide top-of-the-line digital signage devices. INTERNET CONNECTION

Finally, an internet connection is essential to keep your content up-to-date and ensure your screens are always displaying the latest and greatest information. With DigiTAB, you'll have access to real-time updates and the ability to seamlessly manage your content, so you can rest easy knowing your digital signage is always up-to-date and engaging for your target audience.
